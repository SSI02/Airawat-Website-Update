02  Our story

From a campus lab
to national impact

Airawat began with a question asked in the corridors of IIT Kanpur: what if the rigour of frontier AI research could be pointed directly at the everyday friction of urban life?

Incorporated in 2025 as a Section 8 not-for-profit, Airawat now runs the National Center of Excellence for AI in Sustainable Cities — researchers, engineers and domain experts working hand-in-hand with cities and government to ship systems that are intelligent, trusted and built to last.

03  What guides us

Our values

01

Citizen-first

Every line of code is measured against one yardstick — does it make a real citizen's city healthier and easier to live in?

02

Trust & transparency

Explainable AI, secure data handling and open accountability are non-negotiable foundations, not afterthoughts.

03

Measurable impact

We ship outcomes, not dashboards — and hold ourselves to the numbers that matter on the ground.

04

Built to scale

From a single ward to an entire state — our platforms are engineered to grow without breaking.

05

Research-led

Frontier academic research is our engine; real-world deployment is our proving ground.

06

Public good

We exist to strengthen institutions and serve the public interest — that mission comes before everything else.
