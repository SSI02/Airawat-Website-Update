Milestone

The National Center of Excellence for AI in Sustainable Cities is awarded to ARF.

Airawat Research Foundation was entrusted to set up and operate the National Center of Excellence for AI in Sustainable Cities — an initiative under the mission to "make AI in India, and make AI work for India" — anchoring AI research and deployment for urban India at IIT Kanpur.

15 October 2024
