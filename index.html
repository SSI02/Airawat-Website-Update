01  Purpose

We point frontier AI at the everyday friction of urban India — the air, the roads, the power and water, the services.

Airawat Research Foundation is a Section 8 not-for-profit established by IIT Kanpur to run the National Center of Excellence for AI in Sustainable Cities — turning research into systems that cities can actually deploy, and that serve millions of citizens.

03  Approach See the work

How we work

01

Problem-led

We start from the hardest, most measurable challenges a city faces — not from a model looking for a use. The problem decides the method.

02

Indigenous & open

From the ATMAN sensor to open-source transport frameworks, we favour solutions that reduce import dependence and can be adopted widely.

03

Measured by impact

Every thrust is judged by a real-world outcome — cleaner air, safer roads, reliable power, faster services — not by demos alone.
