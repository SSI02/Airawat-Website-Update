Built for everyone

Airawat is committed to building technology that works for everyone. We strive to make our website and platforms accessible to all users, including people with disabilities, in line with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Last updated: 11 June 2026

What we do

  • Provide a light and dark theme, with strong colour contrast for readability.
  • Support full keyboard navigation and clear, visible focus states.
  • Use semantic HTML and descriptive labels for assistive technologies.
  • Respect the "reduced motion" preference to minimise animation for those who need it.
  • Provide meaningful alternative text for images and icons.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is a continuous process. We regularly review our website, test with assistive technologies, and improve based on feedback from our community.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier or have a suggestion, please let us know at accessibility@airawat.org. We aim to respond within five working days.
