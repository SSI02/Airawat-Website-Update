03  The process

How to work with us

(Three steps)

No back-channels — just published scope, an open deadline and a fair read.

01

Watch this page

Open tenders, scope documents and deadlines are published here whenever a notice is active. Check back, or register your email to be notified.

02

Submit your bid

Follow the instructions in each notice to submit a complete, compliant proposal before the stated deadline. Late or partial bids are not considered.

03

Evaluation

Bids are evaluated transparently against the criteria set out in each tender — the same yardstick applied to every applicant.
