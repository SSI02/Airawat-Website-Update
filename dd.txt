      <div data-reveal="" style="translate: none; rotate: none; scale: none; opacity: 0; transform: translate(0px, 46px);">
        <div class="principle">
          <div class="principle__n">01</div>
          <div class="principle__b">
            <h3>Problem-led</h3>
            <p>We start from the hardest, most measurable challenges a city faces — not from a model looking for a use.
              The problem decides the method.</p>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="principle">
          <div class="principle__n">02</div>
          <div class="principle__b">
            <h3>Indigenous &amp; open</h3>
            <p>From the ATMAN sensor to open-source transport frameworks, we favour solutions that reduce import
              dependence and can be adopted widely.</p>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="principle">
          <div class="principle__n">03</div>
          <div class="principle__b">
            <h3>Measured by impact</h3>
            <p>Every thrust is judged by a real-world outcome — cleaner air, safer roads, reliable power, faster services —
              not by demos alone.</p>
          </div>
        </div>
