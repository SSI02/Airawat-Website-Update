Last updated: 11 June 2026

Airawat Research Foundation ("Airawat", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website and platforms, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide directly — such as your name, organisation, email and any message you send us through our forms.
  • Technical information collected automatically — such as device type, browser, and anonymised usage analytics.
  • For deployed platforms, operational data handled strictly under agreements with the relevant public institution.

How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide the services you request.
  • To operate, maintain and improve our platforms and website.
  • To send you updates you have opted in to receive.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the security of our systems.

Data protection & security

We apply industry-standard technical and organisational safeguards — including encryption in transit, access controls and regular audits — to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration or disclosure.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted service providers acting on our behalf, with partner institutions as required to deliver a service, or where required by law.

Your rights

  • Access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you.
  • Withdraw consent or unsubscribe from communications at any time.
  • Raise a concern with us about how your data is handled.

Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, write to us at privacy@airawat.org.
