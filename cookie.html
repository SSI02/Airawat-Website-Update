Cookie Policy

How and why Airawat Research Foundation uses cookies and similar technologies on our website — and the choices available to you.

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Airawat Research Foundation uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website, and the choices available to you.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function and provide information to the site owners.

How we use cookies

  • Essential — required for the website to function, such as remembering your theme preference (light or dark).
  • Analytics — anonymised statistics that help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.
  • Preference — remember choices you make to give you a better, more personal experience.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect how parts of the website function.

Third-party cookies

Some pages may embed content from third parties (such as video), which may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies; please refer to the relevant third party's policy.

Contact

For questions about our use of cookies, write to privacy@airawat.org.
